Privacy Policy for Educational Use of Spatial Blend

Last updated: May 3, 2024

Spatial Blend is dedicated to safeguarding the privacy of our users, particularly in educational contexts. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to ensure the privacy and security of user data.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information for educational purposes:

  • Student Information: In educational settings, we may collect student information such as names, email addresses, and academic progress data solely for educational purposes. This information is collected with the consent of parents or guardians in compliance with applicable laws and regulations, such as COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).
  • Non-personal Information: We may also collect non-personal information such as IP addresses, browser type, and device information for educational analytics and to improve our educational services.

How We Use Information

We use the collected information for the following educational purposes:

  • Educational Services: To provide educational services and resources to students, teachers, and educational institutions.
  • Personalization: To personalize educational content and experiences based on students' learning needs.
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and feedback to improve our educational services and develop new educational resources.

Data Security

We prioritize the security and confidentiality of student information and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include encryption, secure servers, and restricted access to personal data.

Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our educational practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website or through other appropriate means.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or educational practices, please Contact Us.
